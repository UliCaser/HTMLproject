Zaragoza City Council programs a great night show with 200 drones during the Pilar Festival

20/09/2022

That Friday, at 9:15 p.m., a total of 200 aerial vehicles will fly over the Ebro river, between the La Almozara bridge and the Pilar Basilica, for just over a quarter of an hour, offering a choreography dedicated to the festive icons of the city .

100 drones will cross the skies of Hellín in a show to commemorate the 40th anniversary of the Statute of Autonomy of Castilla-La Mancha

17/08/2022

This light show in which more than 100 drones participate can be enjoyed in Hellín this September 23. It will have two screenings at 9:00 p.m. and 10:00 p.m. and will take place at the Santa Ana Sports Complex in Hellinera.
